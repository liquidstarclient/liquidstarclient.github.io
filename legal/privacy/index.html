Legal / Privacy

Privacy,
without the fog.

A short, plain-English summary of what Liquid Star may collect and why. We keep it minimal and use it only to run, verify, secure, and support the service.

Last updated · June 25, 2026

Overview What we collect How it's used Support Sharing Children Contact

Overview

Liquid Star collects as little as it reasonably can. What it does collect is used to run the service, confirm that an account is allowed to access it, keep things secure, and answer support. We do not sell your information and we do not run ads.

What we collect

To provide, operate, and protect Liquid Star, we may process a limited amount of information. This may include the account and access identifiers needed to create, recognise, and verify your access, basic technical and security information from your device, browser, or connection that is used to run the service and protect it against abuse, fraud, and misuse, and any information you choose to give us, such as the contents of a support message. We process only what is reasonably necessary for the purposes set out in this notice.

How it's used

We use that information to provide, maintain, and improve the service, to create and verify accounts and confirm eligibility for access, to keep the service and its users secure and to detect or prevent abuse, fraud, and misuse, to respond to your support requests, and to comply with applicable law. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Support messages

When you contact support, your message and our replies are handled so the conversation can continue across reloads and resist spam. Please don't include passwords, payment details, real-world addresses, or other sensitive information in a support message.

Support records are kept only as long as they are needed and are then removed.

Sharing & third parties

We don't sell or rent your information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us run and protect Liquid Star, or where required by law.

Liquid Star also links to and relies on third parties such as Discord, our hosting and download providers, and your browser. They handle information under their own policies, which we do not control.

Children's privacy

Liquid Star is not directed to children. Younger users should ask a parent or guardian before downloading software, joining Discord, donating, or sending a support message, and should never share real-world personal information.

Retention & changes

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes above or as required by law, then delete or de-identify it. This notice may change as the service changes; the date at the top shows the latest revision.

Questions

Got a privacy question, or want us to clear something on our side? Hop into the Discord and ask — real humans, no ticket-bot runaround.

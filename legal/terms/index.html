What Liquid Star is

Liquid Star is a modding and customization toolkit for your own game client. Its JSUA scripting layer lets you build your own scripts, shaders, visual tweaks, overlays, and other modifications, and share them with others.

Liquid Star also includes optional modules that change how a game plays. These are mods too. Some mods may go against the rules of a game or platform, and we don't encourage using them that way — what you build, enable, and run is your choice, and using any part of it is at your own risk.
